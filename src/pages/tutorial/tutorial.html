eQRScanner

Hey! Thanks for volunteering for Equithon.

First, we need you to give the app permission to use your camera. If you decline, the app won't be able to scan anything D:

After you log in, head over to your profile and check in to your station by selecting the event you're volunteering for on the Status dropdown.

Let's get to work! Hold the scanner up to a QR code until it registers. A popup will appear - if there are any instructions on it, follow them. If not, you just finished checking in that attendee!

Got it? Great! You're ready to go.

If you need any more help, check out the Help page or reopen this tutorial from the side menu.
