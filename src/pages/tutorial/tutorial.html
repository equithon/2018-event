EQRscanner

Hey! Thanks for volunteering for Equithon.

First, we need you to give the app permission to use your camera. Without your approval, the app won't be able to scan anything.

Let's get to work! Make sure you have an event selected, hold the scanner up to a QR code, and hit 'Scan'. A popup will appear - if there are any instructions on it, follow them. If not, you just checked them in!

Got it? Great! You're ready to go.

If you need any more help, check out the Help page or reopen this tutorial from the side menu.
